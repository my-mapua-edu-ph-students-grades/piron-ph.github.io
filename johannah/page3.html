Disaster Preparedness

1.With nothing else to absorb the intensity of storms before they impact land, the Philippines is located just above the equator and faces the western Pacific. The Philippine has the world’s hottest ocean temperature frequently above 28oC which succeeds the first condition in forming a storm, which is a warm ocean that exceeds 26.5 oC . Since the number exceeds the given temperature this gives it a chance for a typhoon to be developed.

2.Before a typhoon happens prepare these to further secure survival: Charge your cell phone and flashlight battery, Ready you Go bag that stores canned food, first-aid kits, drinking water, and pharmaceuticals.

3.During a typhoon remember to remain calm, Do not go out of the house as much as possible, Stay on the news to get updated. If needed, evacuate the needed things to the highest part of the house. Turn off the main switch of the house.

4.After the typhoon follow the given advices don’t go near uprooted trees, Damage structures, and power lines, Stay in your area to avoid causing inconvenience during emergency response, Make sure all electrical appliances are not wet before plugging or opening them.
