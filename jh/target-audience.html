About Us

We are group #7 The only thing we want to get out of this fundraiser is the healthy redemption for the people of sitio bebe. We as students need to know their standing and their hard hardships that even if we hadn’t experienced, we should still appreciate and care for their lives. We are trying to make a difference and we know we can’t solve this problem entirely; but by determination and courage that we want to gain, we will stand our ground and help what we can.
